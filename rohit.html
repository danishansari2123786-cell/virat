About the Legend

Rohit Sharma, fondly known as "The Hitman," is one of the most elegant and destructive batsmen in modern cricket. Currently serving as India's captain across all formats, Rohit has revolutionized the art of opening batting with his ability to convert starts into massive hundreds. His record three double centuries in ODI cricket remain unmatched, showcasing his ability to pace an innings perfectly. As the most successful captain in IPL history with five titles for Mumbai Indians, Rohit combines tactical brilliance with a calm demeanor. His six-hitting prowess and ability to score big runs in crucial matches make him one of the most feared batsmen in world cricket.

Legacy & Impact

Rohit Sharma's impact on cricket extends far beyond his remarkable statistics. He has redefined what it means to be an opening batsman in limited-overs cricket, showing that patience and aggression can coexist beautifully. His ability to accelerate after building a platform has resulted in some of the biggest individual scores in ODI history. As a captain, his calm demeanor and tactical acumen have made him one of the most successful leaders in cricket history, particularly in franchise cricket. His journey from a talented youngster to India's captain across all formats is a testament to his dedication, adaptability, and match-winning ability. The "Hitman" continues to inspire millions with his elegant strokeplay and ability to dominate bowlers in the most stylish manner possible.
